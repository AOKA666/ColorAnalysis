What is AI color analysis?+

AI color analysis examines visible color relationships in a photo, including undertone, value, chroma, and contrast. It then maps those traits to a seasonal palette with practical color recommendations.

How accurate is a personal color analysis from a photo?+

Accuracy depends heavily on the photo. Natural daylight, no filters, minimal makeup, and a visible face give the best result. Screen calibration and artificial light can shift perceived skin tones.

Can I have a neutral skin tone?+

Yes. A neutral skin tone sits between warm and cool, and may lean slightly in either direction. Depth, contrast, and chroma then help identify the most flattering season.

What is the difference between Soft Winter and Deep Summer?+

Both favor some depth and coolness, but Deep Summer is more muted and blended. A soft winter color palette tends to hold slightly clearer contrast and darker jewel tones.

What happens to my uploaded photo?+

This demo processes your image locally in the browser for preview. In a production setup, photos should be encrypted, analyzed transiently, and deleted automatically after processing.
